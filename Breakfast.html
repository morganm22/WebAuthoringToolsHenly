What are we making?

By Tarnejah Rolfe

My favorite time to have this breakfast is in the morning when I wake up. I make this breakfast because it is quick, easy, fulfilling, and delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of dry cereal (of your choice)
  • 1/2 cup of milk (flavor/brand/type of your choice)

What to do

  1. Take small bowl and place on counter
  2. Open cereal box and pour cereal into the bowl
  3. Pour milk on top of cereal
  4. Enjoy with a spoon!
